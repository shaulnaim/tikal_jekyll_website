Tikal Flex Course – Session 2 of 3, coming up on 20/10/09 !


In this session we will be presenting :


  • Flex layout

  • Flex frameworks (Cairngorm, PureMVC)

The course is a closed event to Tikal employees and featured customers only.
This course will be delivered by Ilan Avigdor and

Sefi Ninio, from Tikal Flex Group.

The Flex Course will be given in 3 sessions, 4 hours each, you can sign up to each session separately.

Tikal Flex Course overview:


The course is intended for anyone who wishes to leverage his development skills and master not only the server side, but rich client as well.


The course will deeply cover the important aspects of Flex, and will give you a rock solid foundation to use your skills on top.


All uneducated Flex developers out there consider themselves as Flex developers, doesn't know a fraction of this course content.


It was passed to developers with 1-2 years of Flex development, who were unfamiliar with almost all of it.

You will be able to get Tikal Flex Course Topics: here in a matter of weeks!



Tikal Flex Course Preparations:


All topics are covered from scratch; however a very basic knowledge of Flex syntax and basic stuff (actionScript, MXML, binding) will smooth things up.


This can be acquired in a matter of days (2 to be exact) using this excellent basic video training offered by Adobe: http://www.adobe.com/devnet/flex/videotraining/ (Trust me, most Flex developers are familiar with only this)

Adobe basic video training is a 5 days course:


Days 1-2 : important and relevant to the course

Days 3-4 : Not mentioned in the course, but important for later on

Day 5 : advanced stuff
